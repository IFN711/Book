About The Author

Claire Raylor lives in Brisbane, Australia. She is a registered nurse and has recently ventured into working with children with disabilities. Claire has always been interested in writing and has attended workshops as a hobby. She has previously had some nursing articles published, but to see her early learning books published is a dream come true, especially for such a good cause – a charity changing the lives of others.
